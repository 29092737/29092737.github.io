About the Project: 'CTRL///'was undertaken as a major project in the Year 12 HSC Industrial Technology course. Students who took part in the course were required to submit a major work, accompanying folio and seperate video evidencing the construction of their project. In the division of Industrial Technology: Multimedia, Thomas Howlett (Student 29092737), chose to engage with his audience on the topic of internet security through the medium of a short film/music video, combining a wide range of visual effects and animation elements. Above is the 4 minute long video, accompanying 40 page A3 Folio, 'Behind the Scenes' video and Hand coded website submitted by Thomas to the NSW Education Standards Authority (NESA) for marking.

Post Project Submission: After completion of the course, Thomas was awarded 1st in Industrial Technology based on his internal assesment marks and awarded the College Medal for the subject. His project was also nominated for inclusion in 'InTech' and 'Shape' 2017, an exhibition of a selection of exemplary major projects submitted in the HSC Industrial Technology course in 2017. Post HSC Results, Thomas also received a mark of 98 and a Band 6 for Industrial Technology and ranked 5th for the subject in the state.
